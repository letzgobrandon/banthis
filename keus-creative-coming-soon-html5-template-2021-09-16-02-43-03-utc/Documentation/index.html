Template Installation

  1. FTP Upload:
    • Open up your FTP manager and connect to your hosting
    • Browse to required directory (Normally public_html)
    • Upload the files inside keus-html folder.

Pages for both styles

  • keus-html/style-original/*.html are for default design
  • keus-html/style-particles/*.html are for particles page
  • keus-html/style-particles-2/*.html are for particles 2 page
  • keus-html/style-rain/*.html are for rain page
  • keus-html/style-snow/*.html are for snow page
  • keus-html/style-video-bg/*.html are for video background page
  • keus-html/style-waterpipe/*.html are for waterpipe page
  • keus-html/style-water-ripple/*.html are for waterpipe page
